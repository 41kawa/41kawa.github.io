{: align="center"}
![歓談1](/images/events/025/01.jpg)
![歓談2](/images/events/025/02.jpg)

![ビデオメッセージ](/images/events/025/03.jpg)

![ビンゴ1](/images/events/025/04.jpg)

![ビンゴ2](/images/events/025/05.jpg)
